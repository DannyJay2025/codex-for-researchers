# Sample Deliverable: Citation Support Audit

## Scope

Input type: sample manuscript paragraph.

No live literature search was performed in this sample. Candidate citation needs are described as search targets.

## Claim Table

| ID | Claim | Evidence Needed | Support Status | Safer Wording |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| C1 | AI tools improve research productivity. | Recent empirical study or systematic review | Needs verification | AI tools may improve selected research workflows. |
| C2 | Citation audits reduce reviewer criticism. | Editorial guidance, peer-review study, or writing-method reference | Partial | Citation audits can help identify unsupported or overbroad claims before submission. |
| C3 | Reproducibility packaging is required by all journals. | Journal policy comparison | Overbroad | Many journals increasingly request data and code availability statements. |

## High-Risk Claims

- "All journals" is too broad unless verified against a defined journal set.
- "Improve productivity" needs a measured endpoint.
- "Reduce reviewer criticism" is plausible but should be framed cautiously.
