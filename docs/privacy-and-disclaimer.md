# Privacy And Disclaimer

Codex for Researchers is a workflow aid, not a replacement for expert academic, legal, medical, or institutional review.

## Customer Data

- Do not commit customer files to this public repository.
- Do not publish payment screenshots.
- Do not publish manuscripts, private paper drafts, unpublished data, or reviewer comments.
- Use `orders/`, `customer-files/`, or `private-deliveries/` for local work. These folders are ignored by git.
- Use public examples only when the content is synthetic, public-domain, or explicitly approved after redaction.

## Academic Boundaries

- Do not guarantee acceptance, citation correctness, or compliance.
- Do not fabricate references, statistics, author names, DOI values, PMIDs, or URLs.
- Clearly label abstract-only, excerpt-only, or unverified outputs.
- Encourage customers to verify final claims and citations before submission.

## Suggested Customer Disclaimer

Use this at the end of paid outputs:

```text
Note: This output is an AI-assisted research workflow draft. Please verify all citations, statistics, factual claims, and journal requirements before submission.
```
