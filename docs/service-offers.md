## Recommended Starter Packages

### Citation Safety Check

For authors preparing submission or revision.

Includes:

- Atomic claim split.
- Citation support table.
- Unsupported or overbroad claim list.
- Safer wording suggestions.

Not included:

- Full systematic review.
- Guaranteed acceptance.
- Legal, medical, or regulatory advice.

### Submission Readiness Audit

For research teams preparing a manuscript package.

Includes:

- Manuscript structure notes.
- Citation and overclaim risks.
- Code/data availability statement drafts.
- Reproducibility checklist.

### Private Lab Plugin

For labs that want reusable Codex workflows.

Includes:

- Custom skills for the lab's writing style.
- Field-specific terminology and checklist references.
- Private template pack.
- Onboarding prompts for students and collaborators.

## Delivery Rules

- Never guarantee publication, citation correctness, or compliance without human expert review.
- Mark any unverified citation, statistic, or metadata clearly.
- Keep confidential manuscripts and data out of public examples.
- Use client-approved excerpts only for portfolio material.
- Confirm payment by WeChat screenshot before starting paid work.
- Confirm scope, revision count, and delivery format before accepting payment.
