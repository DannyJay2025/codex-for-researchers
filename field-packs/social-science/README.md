# Social Science Field Pack

## Target Users

- Social science researchers
- Survey and policy research teams
- Graduate students preparing papers or theses
- Consultants reviewing study design claims

## Paid Workflows

- Causal language audit.
- Survey instrument and construct validity review.
- Methods transparency checklist.
- Ethics and data availability wording.

## Field-Specific Risks

- Inferring causality from correlational designs.
- Weak construct definition.
- Missing sampling, exclusion, preregistration, or robustness details.
- Overgeneralizing from narrow populations.

## Premium Assets To Build

- Causal wording ladder.
- Study design checklist.
- Survey validity review template.
- Mixed-methods reporting checklist.
