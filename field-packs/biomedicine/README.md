# Biomedicine Field Pack

## Target Users

- Biomedical researchers
- Clinical translational teams
- Graduate students preparing manuscripts
- Consultants auditing biological or medical claims

## Paid Workflows

- Mechanism claim cautioning.
- Citation support for disease, pathway, biomarker, and intervention claims.
- Data and code availability wording for human-subject or controlled-access data.
- Figure legend and results polishing.

## Field-Specific Risks

- Overstating causality from observational or association data.
- Treating model organism findings as direct clinical evidence.
- Missing ethics, consent, trial registration, or data-access constraints.
- Using review articles to support specific experimental claims.

## Premium Assets To Build

- Mechanism wording ladder.
- Clinical evidence support table.
- Data availability clauses for public, controlled, and unavailable datasets.
- Reviewer-risk checklist for biological claims.
